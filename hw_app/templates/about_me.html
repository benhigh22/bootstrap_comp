Ben High

A scholar, gentleman, and philanthropist, Ben spends the majority of his time saving kittens from trees or babies from burning buildings. Amidst all of his heroics, he still manages to find the time to be a fantasy football enthusiast. He made his billions by destroying all of his competition in every fantasy league ever, then investing his winnings into higher stakes leagues. This cycle went on and on until he one day retired, not from fear of losing but simply to give someone else a chance for once.
